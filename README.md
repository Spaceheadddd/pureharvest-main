# PureHarvest Organics

Official website for **PureHarvest Organics** — a brand rooted in the sacred forests of Araku Valley, Eastern Ghats, India. Raw honey, forest spices, single-origin coffee, and wild dry fruits, sourced directly from indigenous tribal families and brought to the world.

This is a large, interconnected project. What exists here is the foundation — the homepage. Many more pages, portals, and systems are planned and will be built progressively. This repository will be updated continuously as the project grows.

---

## Stack

Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no build tools, no dependencies.

---

## Current State

The homepage (`index.html`) is live and covers:

- Floating header with search, cart, and navigation drawer
- Hero, brand mission, and origin story
- Filterable product collection (Honey, Dry Fruits, Coffee, Spices)
- Category showcase — *From the Wild*
- Featured product with size selector and animated pricing
- Testimonials, Journal preview, B2B & Exports section
- Subscription plans with a 3-step custom modal
- Newsletter capture
- Footer with scroll-driven wordmark animation
